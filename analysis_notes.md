# Analysis Notes

## Objective
Analyze sales data to identify revenue drivers, regional performance, and product demand patterns.

## Key Questions Asked
- What is the total revenue?
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which regions perform best?
- Are high revenues driven by quantity or price?

## Analytical Approach
- Aggregation using SUM
- Grouping by product, category, and region
- Sorting to identify top contributors

## Conclusion
Revenue is primarily driven by high-priced electronics rather than high-volume items.
Business strategy should focus on premium product optimization and region-specific targeting.
